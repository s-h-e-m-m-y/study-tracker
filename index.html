Spaced repetition

Review each lecture at five intervals — Day 1, 3, 8, 14, and 28. Reviews are automatically flagged when they're due based on when you completed the class. This is the most effective thing you can do for long-term retention.

How it works: When you first mark a lecture complete, the clock starts. Each review interval lights up as due on the exact day it falls. Five reviews — 1, 3, 8, 14, and 28 days after the lecture — is enough to push most content into long-term memory. The ⚡ Needs attention section below always shows what's due today.
